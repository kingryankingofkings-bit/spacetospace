Geographic Master Guide - Basic Area Maps Pack

Contents:
- 341 basic area maps (PNG)
- One map for each internal area found across the 36 sub-locations
- Shop and service locations included on the maps
- Every sub-location includes at least one: Inn, Blacksmith, Weapon Skin Shop, and Armor Dye Pack Shop
- Additional shop types are distributed across each sub-location

Files:
- area_maps/ ... all generated area maps by region/sub-location
- area_map_manifest.csv ... index of every map and its included shops
- sub_location_shop_coverage.csv ... per-sub-location coverage check for required shops
