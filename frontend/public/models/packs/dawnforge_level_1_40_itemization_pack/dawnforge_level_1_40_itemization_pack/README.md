# Dawnforge Level 1–40 Itemization Pack

This pack contains a large itemization catalog for **Echoes of the Dawnforge** with armor and weapon pieces spanning required level 1 through required level 40.

## Contents

- **2,200 total equippable pieces**
- **2,000 non-Godly level 1–40 pieces**
- **200 level-40 Godly unique pieces**
- Common through Mythological non-Godly rarity scaling
- Godly rarity reserved exclusively for the 200 level-40 uniques
- Monster drops, random chests, quest rewards, shops, dungeons, bosses, crafting, faction vendors, special events, and world secrets
- CSV, JSON, Markdown, and validation files

## Primary Files

- `ITEMIZATION_SUPPORT_DOCUMENT.md`
- `FULL_ITEM_CATALOG.md`
- `GODLY_LEVEL_40_UNIQUE_CATALOG.md`
- `tables/FULL_ITEM_CATALOG.csv`
- `tables/GODLY_LEVEL_40_UNIQUES.csv`
- `data/full_item_catalog.json`
- `VALIDATION_REPORT.txt`

## Design Rules

Level 1 is Common-only. Legend, Ancient, and Mythological do not appear until after level 20. Ancient starts after level 25. Mythological starts after level 30. The 200 Godly uniques are all required level 40 and are not part of normal random loot tables.
