# Reward Tier and Itemization Guide

This guide defines how quest rewards become stronger without flattening the grind. Story gear is reliable catch-up gear, not the best gear in the game. The strongest rolls, sockets, set refinements, and final affixes should still require crafting, elite encounters, high-combo farm routes, raids, or comparable endgame systems.

## Rarity and Power Bands

| level_range | rarity | design_rule |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1-4 | Common | Basic survivability; teaches armor slots without real build locking. |
| 5-8 | Uncommon | First full armor cycle; no strong set bonuses. |
| 9-12 | Uncommon+ | Adds targeted utility and class-adaptive weapon kits. |
| 13-20 | Rare | Introduces meaningful secondary stats and resistance packages. |
| 21-25 | Rare+ | Late-mid catch-up gear, still below optimized dungeon/crafted items. |
| 26-30 | Epic | First true high-end story gear; requires level gates and upgrade materials. |
| 31-35 | Epic+ | Strong defensive and utility pieces for elite mechanics. |
| 36-39 | Legendary | Pre-capstone story gear; powerful but intentionally socket-limited. |
| 40 | Artifact | Capstone trophies and mastery rewards; no leveling beyond cap. |


## Reward Cadence

- Every quest grants modest XP, a small consumable/material bundle, and one functional reward.

- Every fifth quest tends to provide an armor piece or armor pattern.

- Every tenth quest tends to provide a weapon calibration kit.

- Dungeon and boss quests provide choice caches or trophies but remain bound to the quest's recommended level.

- Every act finale provides a named act relic, an act-set token, and progression unlocks.

- The milestone boss trophy can morph into the appropriate class weapon or focus so reward design does not privilege one class.



## Consumable Scaling

- Levels 1-4: Minor Vitality Injector, Weak Focus Tonic.

- Levels 5-10: Standard Vitality Injector, Combo Salve.

- Levels 11-20: Reinforced Vitality Injector, Guard-Break Oil.

- Levels 21-30: Aether Reconstructor, Overdrive Ampoule.

- Levels 31-39: Eclipse Recovery Flask, Combo-Lock Capsule.

- Level 40: Dawnforge Restoration Core, Astral Guard Flask.
