# Monster Gating and Boss Milestone Support

This document is the encounter-safety companion for the XP and reward matrix. The rule is simple: normal enemies must remain inside the quest's recommended level band; milestone bosses use their explicit milestone level and base XP; no low-level quest should ask players to fight monsters from a higher progression tier.

## Gating Rules

- Levels 1-10 use early monsters only: Grid-Rushers, Gnarl-Maws, Scrap-Phalanx, and Siphon-Ticks.

- Levels 11-25 use mid-game monsters, optionally mixed with early fodder for combo density, but not with late-game elites.

- Levels 26-40 use late-game monsters, introduced gradually: Nexus Swarmers first, then Scrap-Stalkers, then Sylvan Juggernauts, then Void Anomalies.

- Boss XP is encounter XP and is listed separately from quest and subquest XP to prevent double-dipping.

- The Astral Sovereign is the only level-40 capstone gatekeeper in this support pack.
