# Map Coverage Audit — Echoes of the Dawnforge

## Verdict

The current 250-quest storyline does **not** explicitly span the entire uploaded World.txt map set as written. It spans ten narrative campaign regions and their local POIs, but the world atlas contains additional named regions and environmental-combat spaces that are not explicitly required by the questline.

## Correction Applied

This pack adds a mandatory route overlay:

- 250 quest rows mapped to world locations.
- 43 named map locations / structural anchors covered.
- 215 sublocation clusters covered.
- 0 level-gating violations.
- Full atlas coverage achieved by Act 9, with Act 10 reserved for endgame culmination and high-level revisits.

## Files

- `QUESTLINE_MAP_COVERAGE_SUPPORT_DOCUMENT.md` — human-readable support document.
- `QUEST_TO_MAP_COVERAGE_MATRIX.csv` — full 250-row quest-to-location matrix.
- `LOCATION_COVERAGE_LEDGER.csv` — location and sublocation validation ledger.
- `MAP_COVERAGE_VALIDATION_REPORT.txt` — validation summary.
- `data/*.json` — machine-readable versions.
