# Sky Wardens Postgame 100-Quest Storyline — The Order of the Epochalypse Rewrite

## Unlock condition

This secondary storyline unlocks only after the player completes Quest 250 and defeats the Astral Sovereign / final campaign boss.

## Mandatory quest handoff rule

Every quest in this chain is **given by Torinn Rhogar** and **turned in to Torinn Rhogar**. Torinn Rhogar is not created as an NPC asset, not included as a `.glb` file, and not added to the Sky Warden roster in this pack.

## New villain structure

The postgame villains are **The Order of the Epochalypse**, spelled exactly that way. They are a shadow organization attempting to replace the restored post-Dawnforge present with a curated epoch under their control. Each ten-quest arc exposes one Order cell, one lieutenant, one method of temporal/social sabotage, and one piece of the larger Epochalypse Engine plot.

## Reward structure

Each quest awards exactly one unique **Godly** item. Completing all 100 quests grants exactly 10 Godly gear pieces for each of the 10 playable classes.

## XP rule

All quests remain postgame reward quests with **0 XP reward**. The rewards are Godly gear, lore progression, covenant completion, and Sky Warden shadow-ledger advancement.
