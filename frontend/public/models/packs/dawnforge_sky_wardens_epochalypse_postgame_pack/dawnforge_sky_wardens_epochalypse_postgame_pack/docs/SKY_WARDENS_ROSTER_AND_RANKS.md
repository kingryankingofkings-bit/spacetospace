# Sky Wardens Rank Ladder and Roster

## Rank ladder

| Rank | Title | Function |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Cloud Initiate | lowest sworn rank; launch-deck service, messenger duty, emergency rigging |
| 2 | Wind Scout | reconnaissance rank; canopy patrols, glider watch, signal flare duty |
| 3 | Aether Deckhand | shipboard specialist; ballast, sail, engine, and cargo safety |
| 4 | Storm Lancer | frontline aerial infantry; boarding pikes and skyhook assaults |
| 5 | Skybinder | warding technician; aether anchors, rope bridges, and anti-fall fields |
| 6 | Gale Sergeant | squad command; tactical discipline and combat rescue |
| 7 | High Aeronaut | ship officer; navigation through storms, low orbit, and hazard fronts |
| 8 | Cloud Marshal | regional commander; rules a flight wing and arbitration council |
| 9 | Storm Commander | fleet strategist; command authority over multiple ship groups |
| 10 | Sky Warden Prime | highest active rank below the Founder; emergency command authority |
| 11 | Founder | reserved rank only; no active Sky Warden member asset in this pack |

**Rank 11 is reserved for the Founder and is not instantiated as a member asset in this pack.**
