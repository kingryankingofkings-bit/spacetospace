# Sky Wardens Headquarters — Aerie of the Dawnshield

**Model file:** `models/headquarters/SWHQ-001_aerie_of_the_dawnshield.glb`

## Purpose

The Aerie of the Dawnshield is the Sky Wardens' postgame headquarters after the final boss is defeated. It is a floating command fortress, armada dock, oath hall, living garden, and treaty chamber designed to coordinate post-Dawnforge threats without placing the Sky Wardens above the planetary alliance.

## Major Sectors

1. **Highdock Crown** — six exterior docking arms for skiffs, cutters, and frigates.
2. **Stormglass War Room** — rotating command ring for postgame quest briefings.
3. **Aether Thruster Bay** — ship-core maintenance, thruster calibration, and salvage testing.
4. **Cloud Barracks** — rank 1–5 housing, training yards, and patrol staging.
5. **Skyship Hangar** — launch bay for medium and large skyships.
6. **Living Garden Deck** — Sylvan Warden bio-aether recovery space.
7. **Anomaly Watch Gallery** — Protocol Weaver and Phantom-Shift monitoring deck.
8. **Founder Seat Chamber** — an empty rank-11 Founder seat. This is an environmental prop only, not an NPC model.

## Torinn Rhogar Placement

Torinn Rhogar gives and receives every postgame quest in the story documents, but no Torinn model is included in this pack.
