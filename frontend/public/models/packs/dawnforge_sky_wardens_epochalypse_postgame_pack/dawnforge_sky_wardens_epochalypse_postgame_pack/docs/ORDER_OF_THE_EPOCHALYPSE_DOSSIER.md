# The Order of the Epochalypse — Shadow Organization Dossier

## Exact spelling

The villain organization is spelled **The Order of the Epochalypse**.

## Postgame function

The Order of the Epochalypse is the hidden post-Dawnforge antagonist force for the Sky Wardens secondary storyline. The group does not attempt to win by raw conquest. It tries to decide which eras of history are allowed to exist, which witnesses are allowed to remember the final victory, and which institutions are legally permitted to continue after the Astral Sovereign is defeated.

## Core doctrine

The Order believes Veyr's repaired timeline is too unstable to be trusted to free peoples, city councils, Sky Warden captains, living sanctuaries, restored Starwells, or postwar alliances. Its doctrine is called **Curated Ending**: erase the messy present, select a "better" epoch, and crown the Order as the only authorized editor of history.

## Narrative rules

1. The Order must remain a shadow organization through the opening third of the postgame questline.
2. Every quest must expose a concrete Order method, not vague evil.
3. The Order never gives or receives quests. Torinn Rhogar gives and receives all 100 quests.
4. The Order uses evidence redaction, false oaths, future-rot, zero-G desynchronization, phase assassins, chronal salvage, era-weight siege anchors, and thermal paradox engines.
5. The Order should feel intelligent, procedural, and conspiratorial rather than merely monstrous.
6. The final ten quests reveal the Prime Epochalypse Synod and the Epochalypse Engine.

## Design tone

The Order should be presented through clues first: impossible timestamps, witnesses whose testimony rewrites itself, ship manifests from years that have not happened, false Torinn-stamped orders that fail under oath verification, and cult diagrams shaped like overlapping calendars. Direct Order combatants become more common as the chain progresses, but the strongest pressure remains epistemic: the player is fighting to preserve the truth of the world they already saved.
