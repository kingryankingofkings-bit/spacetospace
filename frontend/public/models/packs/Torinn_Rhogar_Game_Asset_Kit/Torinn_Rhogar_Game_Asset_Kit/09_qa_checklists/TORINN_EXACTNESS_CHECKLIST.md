# Torinn Rhogar — Exactness Checklist

Use this checklist before accepting the asset as close to the supplied images.

## Silhouette

- [ ] Dragonborn head is long, square-jawed, noble, and semi-snarled without becoming a generic lizard.
- [ ] Crown/head spikes are layered and visible in profile.
- [ ] Tendrils hang heavily from skull/back and are not replaced by hair.
- [ ] Shoulder skull pauldrons are readable from gameplay camera distance.
- [ ] Robe has long white flowing panels with gold border embroidery.
- [ ] Tail is thick, scaled, and visible in idle and movement.
- [ ] Claws are visible on hands and feet.

## Color and Materials

- [ ] Scales are platinum/silver-white with shimmer, not matte gray.
- [ ] Robe is white/ivory with gold-thread trim.
- [ ] Skull pauldrons are metallic gold.
- [ ] Gems are blue, icy violet, diamond-white, or sapphire-toned.
- [ ] Fist magic is white-blue frost-lightning with blue-green static accents.
- [ ] Aura breath/mist reads as cold, not smoke/fire.

## Detail Accuracy

- [ ] Gold/silver bangles appear near tendril roots.
- [ ] Ring ornaments and precious stones appear near tendril tips.
- [ ] Sash and belt layers wrap around the waist.
- [ ] Gold medallion or skull belt ornament appears at center waist.
- [ ] Bracers/anklets have gold detail and gemstone accents.
- [ ] Robe cloth has layered folds, not one flat sheet.
- [ ] Fist VFX originates from clawed hands, not floating unrelated orbs.

## Gameplay Camera Read

- [ ] In a 1080p gameplay screenshot, Torinn is identifiable by silhouette alone.
- [ ] Gold skull pauldrons remain visible at medium camera distance.
- [ ] Tendrils do not collapse into a single blob.
- [ ] Magic VFX does not obscure the face during idle/combat.
- [ ] White robe is separated from platinum scales by trim, shadow, and material roughness.
- [ ] Aura particles are present but not noisy.

## Canon Lock

- [ ] Height is set to 6'4" / 1.93m.
- [ ] Class fantasy remains monk/sorcerer.
- [ ] Persistent 31°F aura is represented.
- [ ] No ordinary plate armor replaces the monk robe.
- [ ] No red/orange flame magic replaces frost-lightning.
