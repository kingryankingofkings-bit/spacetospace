# Torinn Rhogar — Character Profile and Visual Lock

## Core Identity

**Name:** Torinn Rhogar  
**Species:** Dragonborn male  
**Class fantasy:** Monk / Sorcerer / Divine frost master  
**Height:** 6'4"  
**Role:** Founder-level protector, sacred martial spellcaster, living winter aura source  
**Combat read:** close-range clawed fist combat supported by sorcerous frost, blue-green divine static mist, and disciplined monk movement.

Torinn Rhogar should read as a platinum dragonborn ascetic-warrior, not a conventional armored knight. He is physically powerful, monk-like, regal, and terrifyingly calm. His silhouette must be instantly identifiable: broad dragonborn frame, semi-long square-jawed draconic face, long scaly tendril-hair spilling down his back, white-and-gold monk robes, solid-gold human-skull pauldrons, clawed fists charged with frost-lightning, and a persistent divine cold aura.

## Canon Appearance Lock

Torinn has shimmering platinum scales with a reflective silver-white quality, not flat gray. His face is semi-long and square-jawed with a noble draconic muzzle, layered cheek spines, brow horns, crown-like head spikes, and pale ice-blue eyes. His body is muscular and thick through the chest, shoulders, arms, thighs, and tail, but he must remain monk/sorcerer elegant rather than brute-beast bulky.

His dragonborn “hair” is not hair. It is a heavy mass of thick scaly tendrils hanging from the back and sides of his skull down across the upper back. The tendrils are decorated with gold and silver bangles near the roots and ringed ornaments near the tips. Embedded precious stones should appear blue, sapphire, icy violet, or diamond-white. The tendrils are a major identity feature and should be animated with secondary motion.

## Clothing and Armor

Torinn wears a white tunic with gold-thread trim and layered monk robes. The cloth should be bright white, slightly pearl-toned, and heavily embroidered with gold filigree. The robe silhouette should include wrapped chest folds, a deep waist sash, long front panels, side banners, rear trailing cloth, and movement-friendly lower layers that expose enough leg structure for martial motion.

His shoulders wear solid-gold human-like skull pauldrons. These should be ornate, polished, and sacred-looking, not rusty or necromantic. The skulls are gold trophy-armor, not bone. The eyes and jaw shapes should be readable from gameplay camera distance.

Additional details: gold belt medallion, gold/silver bracers, anklets, jeweled rings, small chains, tassels, and blue gemstone accents.

## Magic and Aura

Torinn channels arcane and divine power through his clawed draconic fists. His magic should not look like generic fire. It should read as frost-lightning: white-blue arcs, blue-green static mist, pale circular glyphs, snowflake sigils, and localized air crystallization around the hands.

His passive divine aura keeps the air within 2 miles at **31°F**. In-game, this should be represented close to the player as breath vapor, light snow flecks, thin ground frost near his feet, pale blue rim lighting, and a faint circular glyph halo during power states. The environment should not automatically become a solid glacier unless the scene specifically calls for it.

## Personality and Presence

Torinn is disciplined, sovereign, controlled, and spiritually severe. He does not posture like a common warlord. His threat comes from stillness, restraint, and the sense that the weather obeys him before he moves. His dialogue should be short, formal, direct, and weighted with command.

## Gameplay Read

Torinn should feel like a high-tier divine monk/sorcerer boss or legendary ally. His primary combat language is hand-to-hand impact, close-range spell channeling, teleport-like monk repositioning, breath-temperature pressure, and defensive aura control.

Essential gameplay silhouette markers:

1. Platinum dragonborn head and long decorated tendrils.
2. White-and-gold monk robe with layered cloth motion.
3. Gold skull pauldrons.
4. Clawed fists surrounded by blue-white frost static.
5. Thick dragon tail with armored/jewelry details.
6. Persistent cold breath/mist and 31°F divine aura.

## Non-Negotiable Visual Rules

- Do not turn him into a standard western dragon knight.
- Do not replace the tendrils with human hair, dreadlocks, feathers, or cables.
- Do not make the skull pauldrons bone-colored; they are solid gold.
- Do not remove the robe silhouette; he is a monk/sorcerer, not a plate-armored fighter.
- Do not make the scales green, red, black, or bronze; his scales are platinum.
- Do not make the fist magic orange/red fire; use frost-lightning, white-blue energy, and blue-green static mist.
- Keep the air cold aura as a constant identity trait.
