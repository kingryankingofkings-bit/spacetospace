Torinn Rhogar is a six-foot-four platinum dragonborn monk/sorcerer whose presence reads as sacred winter made flesh. His shimmering silver-white scales, long square-jawed draconic face, layered crown spikes, pale ice-blue eyes, and heavy decorated scaly tendrils define his silhouette before his robe or magic is even visible. He wears a white tunic and layered monk robe threaded with gold embroidery, bound by a white-and-gold sash and ornamented with polished solid-gold human-skull pauldrons. His tendrils are wrapped in gold and silver bangles near the roots and ringed with gemstone ornaments near the tips. His body is thick, powerful, and disciplined: broad shoulders, clawed hands, heavy legs, taloned feet, and a scaled tail balanced for martial movement.

In combat, Torinn channels divine and arcane power through his clawed fists. The magic around his hands appears as white-blue frost-lightning, pale circular glyphs, snowflake sparks, and blue-green static mist. The air around him is always unnaturally cold: his divinity aura holds the surrounding air at 31°F within a two-mile radius. In game presentation, this should show as breath vapor, soft snow motes, faint ground frost, blue rim light, and restrained glyph halos rather than turning every scene into a glacier.

Torinn should feel calm, ancient, precise, and devastating. He is not a plate-armored knight and not a generic dragon monster. He is a monk/sorcerer with sacred authority, gold death-mask armor, flowing white robes, and fists that make the weather obey.
