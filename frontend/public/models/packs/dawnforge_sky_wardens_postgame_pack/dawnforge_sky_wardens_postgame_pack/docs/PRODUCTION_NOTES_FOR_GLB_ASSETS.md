# Production Notes for Sky Wardens GLB Assets

## Purpose

These assets are procedural blockout / placeholder `.glb` files intended for early prototyping, quest placement, pipeline tests, and implementation references.

## What they include

- Character silhouettes by race.
- Sky Warden uniforms.
- Rank color badges and chest bars.
- Wing-rig or sky-harness elements.
- Race markers such as horns, fins, wings, halos, crystal growths, bark armor, void cores, and stone brows.
- Skyship silhouettes scaled from scout craft to capital carrier.
- A Sky Wardens headquarters environment blockout.

## What they do not include

- Final sculpted anatomy.
- Rigging.
- Animation sets.
- Production-quality topology.
- Final shaders/materials.
- Collision meshes.
- LODs.
- Facial blendshapes.

## Suggested next production pass

1. Import all GLBs into the engine project.
2. Assign placeholder NPC prefabs and skyship prefabs.
3. Replace primitive geometry with final modular art.
4. Add rigging to humanoid NPCs.
5. Add collision proxies to HQ and skyships.
6. Add LOD0/LOD1/LOD2 meshes.
7. Connect roster IDs and quest IDs to the game database.
8. Add Torinn Rhogar separately outside this pack if the design team approves his model creation later.
