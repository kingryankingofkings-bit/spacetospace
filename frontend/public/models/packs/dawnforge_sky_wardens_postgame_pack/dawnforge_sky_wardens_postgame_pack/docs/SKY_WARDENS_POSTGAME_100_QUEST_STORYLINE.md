# Sky Wardens Postgame Storyline — 100 Quest Secondary Campaign

## Unlock condition

This secondary storyline unlocks only after the main campaign has been completed through Quest 250 and the final Astral Sovereign-class capstone gate condition has been cleared. The Sky Wardens interpret the final victory as the start of a dangerous open-sky era rather than the end of conflict.

## Mandatory quest giver rule

**Every quest in this document is given by Torinn Rhogar and turned in to Torinn Rhogar. Torinn Rhogar is referenced only as a quest giver/turn-in and is intentionally not included as a generated NPC asset.**

## Arc overview

### Act I: The Aerie Reopened
- **Purpose:** rebuild the Sky Wardens headquarters after the final boss collapse.
- **Major locations:** The Aerie of Oaths, Oath Ring Observatory, North Skydocks, Aether Beacon, Horizon Barracks.

### Act II: Fleet Above the Broken Starwells
- **Purpose:** recover and recommission the sky fleet scattered across the upper atmosphere.
- **Major locations:** Wisp Runner Docks, Stormcrown Mooring, Cloudlace Dispatch Run, Dawnlift Hauler Bay, Tempest Frigate Yard.

### Act III: Oaths of the Many Races
- **Purpose:** bind the diverse Sky Warden members into a real postwar order.
- **Major locations:** Cloudcourt Hall, Rookery Mess, Wind Pylon Gardens, Aerie Archive, Rescue Deck.

### Act IV: The Storm Vault Campaign
- **Purpose:** hunt remnants and world-sky anomalies left by the Astral Sovereign.
- **Major locations:** Storm Vault Gate, Voidweather Gallery, High Thunder Reaches, Falling Starwell Corridor, Black Cloud Bastion.

### Act V: The Covenant of Open Skies
- **Purpose:** turn the Sky Wardens from a hidden order into the shield of Veyr.
- **Major locations:** Covenant Ark-Carrier, Planetary Overwatch Rail, Open Sky Tribunal, Dawn Beacon Crown, Founderless Oath Spire.
