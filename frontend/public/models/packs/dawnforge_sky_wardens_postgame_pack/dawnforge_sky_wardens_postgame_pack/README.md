# Dawnforge Sky Wardens Postgame Pack

This pack adds a post-campaign Sky Wardens expansion layer for **Echoes of the Dawnforge**.

## Contents

- 50 procedural low-poly Sky Warden NPC `.glb` character files.
- 12 procedural low-poly Sky Warden skyship `.glb` files in varied sizes.
- 1 procedural low-poly Sky Wardens headquarters `.glb` file: **The Aerie of Oaths**.
- 100 postgame quests that unlock after the final-boss clear condition.
- Every postgame quest is **given by Torinn Rhogar** and **turned in to Torinn Rhogar**.
- Torinn Rhogar is intentionally **not created** as a roster member and has **no `.glb` asset** in this pack.
- 100 unique Godly quest rewards: exactly 10 Godly pieces per playable class.
- CSV + JSON import tables and validation report.

## Important asset note

The `.glb` files are procedural, low-poly placeholder assets made for content layout, prototyping, file references, and early pipeline work. They are not final AAA sculpted, rigged, animated, optimized production characters. Each NPC has visible rank color, body/race traits, Sky Warden uniforming, wing-rig styling, and metadata. Replace or refine them in the final art pipeline.
